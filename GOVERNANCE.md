# OSSS Governance

## Roles

### Founding Stewards
Provide guidance on scope, neutrality, and v1 ratification.

### Standards Council
Maintains the specification and approves changes.

### Contributors
Submit issues, RFCs, and pull requests.

---

## Decision Making
- Lazy consensus by default
- RFC + council approval for breaking changes
- Silence implies consent after review period

---

## Transparency
All work occurs in public repositories.
