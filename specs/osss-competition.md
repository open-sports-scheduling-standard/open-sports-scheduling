# OSSS Competition Formats

## Overview
Defines how fixtures are generated or constrained.

---

## Supported Formats

### Round Robin
Parameters:
- cycles
- home_away_mirroring

### Pools / Groups
Defines group membership and play rules.

### Playoffs
Defines dependent fixtures.

Fields:
- dependency_type (winner/loser)
- source_fixture_id

### Tournaments
Supports multi-game-per-day formats.
